Harry Potter VR Spell Dueler

26 Nov 2015

Detail

A virtual reality spell dueler using Google cardboard.

Extended Details

Using **Google cardboard**, we will write a web application that manipulates reality using a smartphone's camera. We will draw over reality spells and their effects for players of the game. We hope to have an N player game, but will shoot for 2 for demoing. **All you should need to play this game is just your smartphone**, Google cardboard, and a wand of your own choosing!
